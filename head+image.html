Spider-Man is a fictional superhero appearing in American comic books published by Marvel Comics. He was created by writer Stan Lee and artist Steve Ditko, first appearing in Amazing Fantasy #15 (August 1962). Spider-Man is the alter ego of Peter Parker, a teenage orphan living in New York City. After being bitten by a radioactive spider, he gains superhuman abilities, including enhanced strength, agility, and the ability to cling to walls. He uses these powers to fight crime as Spider-Man, balancing his superhero life with the challenges of his personal life as a high school student and later, a college student.
